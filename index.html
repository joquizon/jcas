J-cas is a virtual dance droid, that was created to dance. He has 47 points of articulation. His name is an abbreviation of Johnny Castle, a character played by Patrick Swayze in the 1987 blockbuster hit Dirty Dancing. Johnny Castle was a dancing bad boy from the wrong side of the tracks. While J-Cas is no match for the legendary Johnny on the dance floor, like Johnny his love for dance and Baby Houseman knows no bounds. You can check out J-Cas in the Explorer Mode by clicking the eye button or see J-Cas bust some moves in the Dance Mode by clicking the D button.
